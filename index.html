Mesmerizing Nature

ice mountain of Switzerland

The splendid Alps, with different sceneries in the four seasons, occupy almost 60% of Switzerland. In Europe, only Switzerland has so many peaks, including 48 more than 4,000 meters above sea level.

worlds wonderful beach

If you're searching for Southeast Asian beach bliss, super-mellow Saud Beach on the island of Luzon is a sure thing. Its white sand pitches gradually into the clear-as-glass water, like a real-world example of a zero-entry swimming pool.

beautiful waterfall

Waterfalls are one of the most spectacular natural wonders on earth. From the stunning Niagara Falls in North America to the picturesque Angel Falls in South America, waterfalls never fail to amaze and inspire us.
